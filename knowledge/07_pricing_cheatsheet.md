# Pricing & packaging cheat sheet

*Internal · for AE use only — do not share externally*

Last updated: 2026-Q1. Pricing changes quarterly; always confirm via deal desk for sub-€50k offers below list.

---

## Headline structure

Personio is sold per employee per month (PEPM), with a platform fee, plus modules.

- **Platform fee** — covers core HRIS, employee records, time off, document management
- **Module fees** — Payroll, Performance, Recruiting, Time Tracking
- **Implementation fee** — one-time, scoped per deployment

Annual contract, billed annually in advance. Multi-year discounts available.

Implementation timelines: 6 – 10 weeks core, 8 – 16 weeks with payroll. Don't promise faster without IM sign-off.

## Discounting guidelines

**You can do (no approval needed):**
- Up to 10% off platform list price for 2yr commitment
- Up to 15% off platform list price for 3yr commitment
- Waive setup fee for deals over €100k ARR

**Needs deal desk:**
- Discounts beyond the above
- Custom payment terms (anything other than annual upfront)
- Free months / ramp pricing
- Logo / case study exchange offers

**Don't ever:**
- Discount on first call. Anchor to list. Concessions come after value is established.
- Discount without trade. Every concession must come with something.
- Promise implementation pricing before solutions engineering scopes it.

## Common pricing scenarios

### Customer pushes "we got X% off from competitor"

Don't match. "Our pricing reflects what we deliver. Where we can be flexible is on commitment length, scope phasing, or implementation. Want to walk through those?"

### Customer wants to start small and expand

Reasonable, common ask. Structure it as:
- Year 1: HRIS + 1 module
- Year 2: + 1 module (committed at year 1 signing, priced at year 1 rates)
- Pre-commit gives them price certainty; us a longer commitment.

Don't do "Year 1 cheap, year 2 we'll figure it out" — that's how renewals blow up.

### Customer is borderline ICP and very price-sensitive

Pull deal desk in early. Often we have a path (Essential tier, partner referral, longer implementation timeline in exchange for lower price). What we won't do is engineer-around to make a bad-fit deal close.

### Customer asks for monthly billing

We don't do monthly. We can do quarterly billing in advance for deals over €100k ARR (deal desk approval).

## Things candidates often miss

- The platform fee is *not* the full price. Modules add up fast. A 500-employee customer with all four modules is €18–22 PEPM, not €10.
- Multi-country payroll is *not* one fee — pricing is per country, additive.
- Implementation is usually 1–2 months of ARR equivalent. Customers anchored on monthly SaaS pricing get surprised by this. Set expectation early.

---

## When to escalate to deal desk

- ACV > €150k
- Any non-standard term
- Discounts beyond your authority
- Multi-entity / multi-country structuring
- Government, public sector, regulated industries
- Renewal at risk and price-sensitive

Deal desk responds within one business day. Don't promise pricing in the room without confirming.
